Data Analytics Project

    During the 2020/2021 school year I completed a data analytics project for the New Revenue & Innovation Practice Director of Horizontal Digital. I used Einstein Analytics, which is a feature of Salesforce. I found a data set on Kaggle, formatted and trimmed the data, and inputted it into Eintsein Analytics. This software uses machine learning to maximize or minimize a chosen variable. For my dataset, I wanted to maximize the positive response of health insurance customers, when their insurance provider attempted to sell them car insurance as well. I put together a presentation for the hypothetical insurance company, highlighting which demographics are most likely to respond positively, and which demographics are not being communicated with in the most effective way.
